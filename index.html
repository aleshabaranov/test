Mambo vipi? Wacha nikupatie story yangu uone kama itakusaidia, especially kama umesumbuka na chapaa kama mimi, uzuri mi nilitoboa! Would you like to know how?

I was able to make money on a trading platform, I won’t mention the names, so as not to advertise, those who are interested can download the app in Google Play .

Maisha Before

Me ni msee wa boda, na ile chapaa me hutengeneza iko affected na rides me hupata daily. Actually, Job hukua, but the competition from other boda-boda riders does not allow transporting enough people for my income to cover at least my basic living expenses. Situation ni balaa zaidi since nilichukua loan kubuy boda. I thought that I could quickly pay off that loan and start saving money. I was dreaming... in reality my earnings were only enough to cover the monthly loan payments plus interest and of course food. But I was still a young man, and I had a girl whom I wanted to marry. Both ku date na kuoa requires some money. So nilianza kufikiria of ways to get extra income.

The Search

Niliangalia options nyingi, which turned out to be unsuitable. Some opportunities required an investment, na sikuwa na extra cash. Others needed long hours of work, but I couldn’t quit hustle yangu. Finally, I came across the source ya kutengeneza extra income by accident. Nilikutana na beshte wa before, who was once unemployed and in great need. Beshte yangu alikua anakaa ontop, judging by his appearance it seemed alitoboa life. I asked how he managed to earn a good living. Akaniambia about trading using the trading platform.

Nika-decided to give it a try, because I had nothing to lose. Hiyo platform inapatia beginners a free demo account with 100K balance, which I used. Nikwambie aje? There are ups and downs, but I always have some money saved up for a rainy day, and thanks to the free training materials provided by the service, nilieza ku-trade more and more successfully with each try.

Happy Ending

Bado me sijafikia kuwa super rich man, but I didn’t expect it to happen so quick anyway. At least naeza buy my girlfriend gifts and take her to the movies. Nimeweka savings for our wedding. Chenye napenda the most is the convenience of trading: there is a user friendly interface and detailed training materials. Kitu muhimu ni kutumia knowledge wisely, and, of course, having good luck helps. I am quite pleased that I retained my main source of income (I really didn’t want to lose it) and I always have some extra income coming in from trading.
